Bare Habits

Focus on what matters.

Build better habits, one day at a time, without the clutter. Bare Habits is a clean, focused habit tracker for people who value simplicity, consistency, and calm progress.

Daily Focus

Check off your habits with a single tap. The 7-day flow keeps attention on today while making it easy to catch up on recent progress.

Weekly Overview

See the bigger picture at a glance. The weekly view helps you understand consistency across all your habits without adding noise.

Insightful Stats

Track streaks and completion rates clearly with lightweight charts that stay useful without taking over the experience.

Daily Reminders

Set a reminder at your preferred time so the app supports your routine quietly, rather than competing for attention.

Refined Experience

  • Minimalist aesthetic that keeps the focus on the habits themselves.
  • Vibrant haptics that make completion feel satisfying without being distracting.
  • Light-mode clarity tuned for readability and fast scanning.
  • Liquid Glass-inspired interface designed to feel polished, modern, and calm.
