Bare Habits

Focus on What Matters.

Build better habits, one day at a time, without the clutter. Bare Habits is a clean, focused habit tracker designed for those who value simplicity and consistency. No distractions, just your progress.

✨ Daily Focus

Check off your habits with a single tap. An elegant 7-day navigation keeps you oriented and focused on today, while making it easy to catch up on recent progress.

📅 Weekly Overview

See the big picture. The comprehensive weekly view helps you visualize your consistency across all your habits simultaneously.

📊 Insightful Stats

Track your journey with clarity. Monitor your streaks and completion rates at a glance with beautiful, non-intrusive charts.

🔔 Daily Reminders

Stay on track without the stress. Set a custom daily reminder at your preferred time to ensure you never miss a beat in your habit-building journey.

🎨 Refined Experience

  • Minimalist Aesthetic: A design that breathes, letting you focus on your goals.
  • Vibrant Haptics: Subtle feedback that makes marking a habit complete feel rewarding.
  • Light Mode Optimized: A crisp, clear interface for maximum readability.
  • Liquid Glass: Designed for iOS 26, Liquid Glass provides a modern, glassy interface that makes the app feel like it's part of your daily routine.
