About Me

I am a doctoral student at the Perception and Intelligence Lab at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy, under the guidance of Prof. Fabio Galasso. My research centers on deep learning and computer vision, with focus on self-supervised, unsupervised, and active learning leveraging hyperbolic neural networks.

Currently, I am collaborating with Panasonic North America AI Lab and great researchers from BAIR on cutting-edge research on active domain adaptation for semantic segmentation and image-text interaction in hyperbolic space.

I received my Master's degree in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome in 2021, graduating with honors and a grade of 110/110. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Roma Tre University, Italy (2019). For my Bachelor's thesis, I had the privilege of interning at Xpeppers where I developed a serverless application for monitoring event-driven architectures on AWS.
