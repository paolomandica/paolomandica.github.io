I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, where I am part of the Perception and Intelligence Lab Italy, led by Prof. Fabio Galasso.
My research centers on deep learning and computer vision, with focus on self-supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and active learning leveraging hyperbolic neural networks. Some of my research interests and hands-on experience include: action recognition, domain adaptation, and semantic segmentation.

I received my Master's degree in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome in 2021, graduating with a grade of 110/110 cum laude. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Roma Tre University, Italy (2019). For my Bachelor's thesis, I interned at Claranet IT where I developed a serverless application for monitoring event-driven architectures on AWS.
