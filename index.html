Paolo Mandica

Hello dear visitor! I'm a 2nd year Ph.D. candidate at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy, under the guidance of Prof. Fabio Galasso. My research focuses on Self-supervised Learning and Active Learning using Hyperbolic Neural Networks.

Currently, I am interning at Panasonic R&D AI Laboratory, located in Mountain View, California. During my internship, I am focusing on exploring Active Domain Adaptation for Semantic Segmentation using Hyperbolic neural networks.

In 2021, I received my Master's degree in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome, graduating with honors and a grade of 110/110. My Master's thesis was titled "Self-supervised Representation Learning via Contrastive Walks in Videos". I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Roma Tre University, Italy, in 2019. During my Bachelor's thesis, I had the privilege of interning at Xpeppers, where I developed a Serverless Application for Monitoring Event-driven Architectures on AWS.
