Paolo Mandica

I'm a 2nd year Ph.D. student at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy, under the guidance of Prof. Fabio Galasso. My research focuses on Self-supervised Learning and Active Learning using Hyperbolic Neural Networks.

Currently, I am interning at Panasonic R&D AI Laboratory in Mountain View, California. As part of my internship, I am engaged in cutting-edge research on Active Domain Adaptation for Semantic Segmentation, working in collaboration with great researchers from BAIR (Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research).

I received my Master's degree in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome in 2021, graduating with honors and a grade of 110/110. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Roma Tre University, Italy (2019). For my Bachelor's thesis, I had the privilege of interning at Xpeppers.
